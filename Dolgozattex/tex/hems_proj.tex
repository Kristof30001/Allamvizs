\begin{abstractEN}
This updated report extends the original HEMS benchmark by integrating real day-ahead electricity prices (ENTSO-E, RO zone), reorganizing experiment outputs, and adding richer visual analytics. The optimization core remains a comparative evaluation of nature-inspired metaheuristics (GA, PSO, GWO, Hybrid), while the input realism is improved through real PV day profiles and real hourly price signals. The updated pipeline stores outputs in structured folders (tables/reports/plots), generates day-level and summary PNG figures, and preserves reproducibility through fixed seeds. Results indicate that ranking remains scenario-dependent, but robustness conclusions are now better supported by clearer reporting artifacts. This document keeps the original thesis structure and adds a dedicated chapter summarizing modifications, current status, and immediate next steps.
\end{abstractEN}
